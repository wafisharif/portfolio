About Me

I'm a high school researcher and engineer passionate about physics, robotics, and computational modeling. I build interactive physics simulators, conduct materials and biomechanics research, and design complex robotic systems through CAD, electronics, and software integration.

What I Do

  • Materials Science and Mechanical Systems Research: Investigating sustainable binder chemistry for lithium-ion batteries through electrochemical characterization and material synthesis.
  • Bio-Inspired Robotics: Developing penguin-inspired locomotion models in OpenSim and validating them with physical robot experiments.
  • FIRST Tech Challenge Robotics #19426: CAD design, prototyping, machining, and system integration for competitive FTC robots.
  • AP Physics Guide & Physics Simulators: An online educational platform teaching physics concepts in the form of slideshows and animations, and a suite of JavaScript/p5.js tools modeling AP Physics concepts including SHM, projectile motion, circular motion, collisions, and more.
  • Maker Projects: Arduino systems, mechanical prototypes, custom electronics, and engineering experiments.
